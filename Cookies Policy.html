Cookies Policy

Opal Life Initiative - Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 1-Jan-2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience and understand how our site is being used.

2. How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session management
  • Website security
  • Basic functionality

Analytics Cookies

We use these to understand how visitors interact with our site:

  • Page visits and navigation
  • Time spent on pages
  • Geographic location (country level only)

Preference Cookies

These remember your choices:

  • Language preferences
  • Accessibility settings
  • Consent preferences

3. Your Choices

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our site, you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Choose specific cookie categories
  • Reject non-essential cookies

Managing Cookies

You can change your preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer
  • Adjusting your browser settings
  • Clearing cookies through your browser

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use limited third-party services that set cookies:

  • Google Analytics (anonymous usage data)
  • Social media platforms (if you interact with our share buttons)

5. Data Protection

We are committed to your privacy:

  • We only use cookies for legitimate purposes
  • We don't sell your data to third parties
  • We comply with applicable data protection laws

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be:

  • Posted on our website
  • Dated with the latest revision
  • Communicated through our newsletter (if subscribed)

7. Contact Us

For questions about our cookie policy:

8. Additional Information

  • We don't use cookies for advertising purposes
  • Cookies typically expire after 30-365 days
  • You can use our website without accepting non-essential cookies

Thank you for supporting Opal Life Initiative's mission while we respect your privacy choices.
