Privacy Policy - Opal Life Initiative

Last Updated:1-Jan-2025

1. Introduction

Opal Life Initiative ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website, programs, and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, donation history, volunteer applications
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, device information, website usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process donations and communicate about their impact
  • Manage volunteer programs and events
  • Send updates about our initiatives and impact reports
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Partner organizations for joint initiatives (with your consent)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure servers and encryption
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security assessments

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for:

  • Fulfilling the purposes outlined in this policy
  • Complying with legal requirements
  • Maintaining business records

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent. Our youth programs include appropriate privacy safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be:

  • Posted on our website
  • Communicated to subscribed users
  • Dated with the latest revision

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests.

12. Consent

By using our services, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Thank you for trusting Opal Life Initiative with your information as we work together to create positive change in our communities.
