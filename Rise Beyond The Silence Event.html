Rise Beyond The Silence

Rise Beyond Silence – Women’s Empowerment and GBV Awareness Workshop
Date: August 5th, 2024
Location: Umoja Estate

Workshop Sessions and Discussions

  • Understanding different forms of gender-based violence (GBV)
  • Legal rights and navigating the justice system
  • Building self-esteem and personal empowerment
  • Accessing support services and resources
  • Addressing harmful cultural norms and practices
  • Breaking the silence around survivor experiences

Interactive Activities

  • Role-playing exercises for real-life scenarios
  • Group discussions on community-specific challenges
  • Educational skits demonstrating healthy relationships
  • Survivor storytelling sessions (voluntary participation)
  • Allyship training for male participants
  • Counseling and support group formations

Skill-Building Workshops

  • Communication and assertiveness training
  • Conflict resolution techniques
  • Self-defense demonstrations
  • Digital safety and privacy protection
  • Economic empowerment strategies
  • Community resource mapping

Support Services Provided

  • One-on-one counseling sessions
  • Legal advice and consultation
  • Healthcare information and referrals
  • Safe space facilitation
  • Crisis intervention resources
  • Follow-up support planning

Community Engagement Activities

  • Men's allyship sensitization session
  • Intergenerational dialogue circles
  • Community champion identification
  • Local leader engagement
  • Network building among participants
  • Resource material distribution

Ceremonial Activities

  • Candlelit affirmation ceremony
  • Solidarity pledge commitment
  • Community protection promises
  • Healing and closure rituals
  • Group empowerment celebrations
  • Survivor recognition moments

Educational Components

  • GBV prevention strategies
  • Legal framework education
  • Health and wellness information
  • Safety planning techniques
  • Community response protocols
  • Ongoing learning resources
